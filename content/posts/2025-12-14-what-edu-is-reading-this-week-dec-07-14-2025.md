Here is the roundup of links for this week:

## Cloud & Infrastructure

* [**IBM to acquire Confluent**](https://www.confluent.io/blog/ibm-to-acquire-confluent/) - Major industry news as IBM announces its acquisition of the data streaming platform Confluent.
* [**Netvisor**](https://github.com/netvisor-io/netvisor?ref=selfh.st) - An open-source observability agent for Kubernetes that provides visibility into cluster traffic and network calls.
* [**Kubeterm**](https://github.com/kbterm/kubeterm?tab=readme-ov-file) - A graphical terminal specifically designed for managing Kubernetes clusters.
* [**Ingress Nginx Migration**](https://ingressnginxmigration.org/) / [**GitHub Repo**](https://github.com/traefik/ingress-nginx-migration): A tool to assist in migrating from the Nginx Ingress Controller to Traefik.

## DevOps & Tools

* [**Cloudflare Error Page**](https://github.com/donlon/cloudflare-error-page?tab=readme-ov-file) - A project for creating custom, branded error pages for Cloudflare deployments.
* [**DevIQ**](https://deviq.com/) - A comprehensive resource and reference guide for software design patterns, principles, and practices.
* [**OWASP crAPI**](https://owasp.org/www-project-crapi/) / [**GitHub Repo**](https://github.com/OWASP/crAPI): The "Completely Ridiculous API" is an intentionally vulnerable application designed by OWASP for teaching API security.
* [**12 Days of Command Line**](https://12days.cmdchallenge.com/) - A holiday-themed set of command line challenges to test your terminal skills.

## AI & Engineering

* [**How AI is transforming work at Anthropic**](https://www.anthropic.com/research/how-ai-is-transforming-work-at-anthropic) - A look into how Anthropic uses its own AI models internally to change their workflows.
* [**Effective Harnesses for Long-Running Agents**](https://www.anthropic.com/engineering/effective-harnesses-for-long-running-agents) - An engineering post from Anthropic on building test harnesses for autonomous AI agents.
* [**Recreating the Space Jam Website with AI**](https://theahura.substack.com/p/i-successfully-recreated-the-1996) / [**Failing to Recreate it**](https://j0nah.com/i-failed-to-recreate-the-1996-space-jam-website-with-claude): Two contrasting perspectives on attempting to recreate the 1996 Space Jam website using Claude.

* [**Wayland & Nvidia: A Definite 2025 Guide**](https://kextcache.com/wayland-nvidia-a-definite-2025-guide/) - A guide to configuring and running Wayland on Nvidia hardware in 2025.
* [**Hidden Microphone on NanoKVM**](https://telefoncek.si/2025/02/2025-02-10-hidden-microphone-on-nanokvm/) - A security finding revealing an undocumented microphone inside the NanoKVM hardware.
